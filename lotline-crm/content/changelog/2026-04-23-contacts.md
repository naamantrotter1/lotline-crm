# Contacts

The Contacts module is now live. Manage sellers, buyers, investors, attorneys, contractors, and more — all in one place.

**What's new:**
- `/contacts` — full contact list with table + Kanban-by-lifecycle views
- Search and filter by type or lifecycle stage
- `/contacts/:id` — profile, timeline, notes, and linked deals
- Multi-type tagging (seller, buyer, investor, attorney, and more)
- Lifecycle stages: New → Working → Qualified → Customer → Dormant
- Quick-add from anywhere with the `+` button in the top bar (or press `C`)
- Deal linking with role (seller, buyer, investor, etc.)
- Per-org RLS — contacts are fully isolated by organization
